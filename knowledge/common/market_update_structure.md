# Market Update Report Structure

## Sections (in order)

1. **Executive Summary** — one-page overview of all three segments and macro context.
2. **Macroeconomic Overview** — GDP, inflation, BI rate, IDR/USD, JCI.
3. **Reinsurance Market** — industry-level KPIs, company-by-company highlights.
4. **General Insurance Market** — industry-level KPIs, key line-of-business breakdown.
5. **Life Insurance Market** — industry-level KPIs, product-mix commentary.
6. **Appendix** — data tables, sources, methodology notes.

## Periodicity
- Published quarterly (Q1 = April, Q2 = July, Q3 = October, Q4 = January).
- Data cut-off: last available published financial statement for the reference quarter.

## Audience
- Internal: Indonesia Re management and board.
- External: select clients and counterparties (toned for sophisticated insurance readers).
