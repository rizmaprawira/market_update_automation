# Report Writing Style Guide

## Tone
- Formal, analytical, and objective.
- Third-person throughout; avoid "we", "our", "I".
- Present findings first, then interpretation.

## Numbers
- Currency: IDR in billions (Rp triliun in Bahasa Indonesia sections).
- Always state YoY or QoQ growth direction explicitly: "increased by X% YoY".
- Round to one decimal place for ratios; zero decimals for large IDR figures.

## Structure of Each Section
1. **Opening sentence** — headline finding (most significant KPI movement).
2. **Supporting data** — two to three sentences with specific numbers.
3. **Interpretation** — what the data implies for the market / company.
4. **Outlook** (optional) — one sentence forward-looking commentary if warranted.

## Tables
- Label every table with a caption and unit (e.g., "IDR billion").
- Include a period column and a YoY change column.

## Language
- Indonesian terms should be italicised on first use, followed by English translation in parentheses.
- Avoid jargon not defined in `common_insurance_terms.md`.
