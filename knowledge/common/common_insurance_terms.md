# Common Insurance Terms

Shared glossary used by all analyst agents when writing narrations.

## Premiums
- **Gross Premium Written (GPW)** — total premium booked before deducting reinsurance ceded.
- **Net Premium Written (NPW)** — GPW minus ceded reinsurance premium.
- **Earned Premium** — portion of written premium attributable to the expired portion of the policy period.

## Ratios
- **Loss Ratio** — net claims incurred / net earned premium. Measures underwriting loss experience.
- **Expense Ratio** — underwriting expenses / net earned premium.
- **Combined Ratio** — loss ratio + expense ratio. Below 100% = underwriting profit.
- **Benefit Ratio** (life) — benefits paid / gross premium.

## Balance Sheet
- **Technical Reserves** — liabilities set aside to pay future claims.
- **Risk-Based Capital (RBC)** — regulatory solvency ratio; OJK minimum is 120%.

## Indonesian Regulatory Context
- **OJK** — Otoritas Jasa Keuangan, Indonesia's financial services regulator.
- **AAJI** — Asosiasi Asuransi Jiwa Indonesia (life insurance association).
- **AAUI** — Asosiasi Asuransi Umum Indonesia (general insurance association).
