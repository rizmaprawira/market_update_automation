# Chart Style Guide

## Dimensions
- Default figure size: 10 × 6 inches at 150 dpi.
- Export format: PNG for Word/PDF embedding; SVG optionally for web.

## Colours
- Indonesia Re brand primary: `#003366` (navy blue)
- Accent 1: `#E8A020` (gold)
- Accent 2: `#C0392B` (red — use for negative / loss metrics)
- Neutral: `#7F8C8D` (grey — reference lines, prior periods)

## Typography
- Title: bold, 13 pt
- Axis labels: 10 pt
- Data labels: 9 pt
- Legend: 9 pt, outside plot area (right or bottom)

## Required Elements
- Chart title (include period in title, e.g., "GPW Growth — Q1 2026")
- Axis labels with units
- Source note at bottom: _Source: [company name / OJK], [date]_
- Watermark: "Indonesia Re Research" bottom-right (light grey, 8 pt)

## Chart Types by Use Case
- Time-series trends: line chart
- Period comparisons (two periods): clustered bar
- Portfolio / mix: stacked bar or pie (pie only for ≤5 categories)
- Ratio metrics: bar with reference line at 100%
